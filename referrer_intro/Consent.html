Study description

Researchers from the European University Institute and the University of Amsterdam request your participation in a study concerning performance in a maths task and an empathy task.

You will be paid a flat fee of {{ Constants.participation_fee }} to participate. You will also receive a bonus payment of up to {{ Constants.reward_bonus }}, based on your evaluation of the players’ performance.

This study has been approved by the European University Institute’s Ethics Review Committee.


Data processing

Data will be collected on your performance, as well as some basic demographic information which you have already provided to Prolific. All data are stored anonymously and cannot be linked to specific names.

Data will be stored on servers located in the European Union. This data will be analysed statistically by the researchers and is intended for use in an academic publication. It may also be made available to the international research community, for transparency and re-usage in future studies, which may involve international data transfers.
